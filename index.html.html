ABOUT

LoveMeSomeArt.com Is an art platform built to showcase and facilitate collection and sale of works by outstanding emerging and mid-career artists.

We bring to fore emerging and mid-career artists with a cultivated visual art practice from the South Asian region with the aim to help collectors’ broaden their lens, discover exciting culturally relevant artists on the rise; where collectors’ get to know the art they buy and the artist they buy from.

At LMSA we aim to champion, develop and showcase the works of outstanding artists on the rise.
